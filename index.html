What We Offer

Comprehensive Dental Services

From routine family check-ups to advanced cosmetic and restorative procedures, we offer the full spectrum of dental care under one roof.

Dental implants

Dental Implants

A titanium fixture placed into the jawbone to replace missing teeth — restoring your natural smile, chewing function, and preserving the jawbone.

Crowns & bridges

Crowns & Bridges

Caps and bridges that protect weakened teeth, replace large fillings, restore fractures, and hide discolouration — permanently cemented for lasting results.

Cosmetic dentistry

Cosmetic Dentistry

Expert cosmetic treatments to enhance your smile. We create personalised treatment plans tailored to your aesthetic goals and budget.

Children's dentistry

Children's Dentistry

Fun, pain-free check-ups in a welcoming environment — building healthy dental habits from an early age for Bega Valley families.

Dentures

Dentures

Partial, complete, or implant-supported dentures that improve aesthetics, restore chewing function, and protect your remaining teeth.

Clear aligners

Clear Aligners

Discreet, removable aligners for minor tooth corrections — a virtually invisible path to a straighter, more confident smile.

Zoom whitening

Philips Zoom Whitening

Professional in-chair whitening that brightens your smile by up to eight shades in a single visit — enamel-safe, clinically proven.

3D scanning technology

Trios 3D Scanner

Our digital Trios 3D scanner replaces physical impressions — more comfort, better accuracy, faster results for implants, aligners, and restorations.

Modern Care

Technology & Comfort, Together

Trios 3D digital scanning suite

Advanced Technology

Trios 3D Digital Scanner

Say goodbye to uncomfortable physical impressions. Our Trios 3D scanner captures a precise digital model of your teeth in minutes — improving comfort, accuracy, and turnaround for implants, crowns, bridges, and aligners.

  • No messy impression material
  • Higher accuracy than traditional moulds
  • Faster lab turnaround times
  • Full digital workflow for implants & restorations
