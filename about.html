Welcome

A Trusted Name in Bega Valley Dentistry

Bega Dental Surgery & Laboratory has cared for families across the Bega Valley from the same main-street location for more than a century. Over that time our commitment has stayed the same: quality, family-oriented dental care delivered in a modern, friendly environment.

We provide the full range of general dental care — with a strong focus on preventive oral health and honest, straightforward advice. Our goal is to help every patient, of every age, keep a healthy smile for life, and to make each visit as comfortable and reassuring as possible.

You'll find us conveniently located on Bega's main street. New patients are always welcome — call us on (02) 6492 1019 to arrange your first appointment.
